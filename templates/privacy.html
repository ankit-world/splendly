Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information: your name and email address provided at registration.
  • Expense data: amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter into the app.
  • Usage data: pages visited and actions taken within the app, used to improve the Service.

We do not collect payment information, government IDs, or any sensitive financial credentials.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To display your expense history and spending summaries.
  • To diagnose bugs and improve the reliability of the Service.

We will never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secured servers. We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your information against unauthorised access, loss, or disclosure.

You may delete your account at any time from your profile settings. Upon deletion, all associated personal data and expense records will be permanently removed within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly uses a small number of third-party services to operate:

  • Google Fonts — to load the typefaces used in the interface. Google may log the font request; no personal data from your account is shared.

We do not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or social login providers. If this changes, this policy will be updated accordingly.

5. Cookies

Spendly uses a single session cookie to keep you logged in between visits. This cookie contains no personally identifiable information and is deleted when you log out or close your browser session.

We do not use tracking cookies or third-party cookies.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out via the contact information provided in your account settings. We aim to respond within 5 business days.
